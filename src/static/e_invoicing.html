Electronic invoicing portal

STC e-invoicing lets registered taxpayers enroll devices, submit signed UBL invoices, clear taxable invoices, and report invoices through a structured API flow. The server validates schema, invoice hashes, XAdES-BES signatures, certificates, taxpayer TINs, and invoice chain state.

1. Sign in Enter a registered taxpayer TIN and password.
2. Enroll device Generate a short-lived token and enroll a CSR.
3. Submit invoices Test clearance and reporting in the sandbox.
