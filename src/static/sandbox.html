Validation-only sandbox behavior

All invoice submissions go through /sandbox/invoices/* endpoints. The server validates XML schema, invoice hash, XAdES-BES signature, certificate chain, enrolled device, and taxpayer TIN rules, but successful and failed submissions are not persisted and ICV/PIH chain state is not mutated.
