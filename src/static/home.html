Types of Taxes

Core tax categories and services provided through the Taxation Chamber.

Value Added Tax (VAT)

Tax applied to the value added through supply chains for eligible goods and services.

Income Tax

Taxation for income generated by individuals and organizations under current laws.

Capital Gains Tax

Tax procedures covering gains from the disposal or transfer of capital assets.

Stamp Duty Tax

Duty applied to selected documents, contracts, and regulated transactions.

About Taxes

Taxes are a vital source of public revenue for the state. They contribute to financing public services, supporting development projects, and shaping financial and economic policy.
